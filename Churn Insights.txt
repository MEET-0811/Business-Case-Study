 Example Insights with Questions & Answers
1. Are younger customers more likely to churn?
 Answer:

Yes, younger customers showed a higher churn rate in the dashboard analysis. Most churned customers belonged to lower age groups and had lower session activity compared to older customers.

2. Is monthly subscription less stable than yearly subscription?
 Answer:

Yes, monthly subscription customers had the highest churn rate. Yearly subscribers were more loyal because they showed higher engagement and more active sessions.

3. Is low session attendance a churn predictor?
 Answer:

Yes, customers with fewer gym, yoga, or consultation sessions were more likely to churn. Low activity indicated less engagement with the membership services.

4. Are low-feedback customers more likely to leave?
 Answer:

Yes, customers with low feedback scores had a higher churn probability. Poor feedback indicated dissatisfaction with gym services and customer experience.

5. Which subscription type had the highest customer retention?
 Answer:

Yearly subscriptions had the highest retention rate. These customers remained more active and had better feedback scores compared to monthly users.

6. How does customer engagement affect churn?
 Answer:

Higher customer engagement reduced churn significantly. Customers who attended more sessions and logged in regularly were less likely to leave the membership program.
